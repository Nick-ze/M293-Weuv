Receiving a Serve

Receiving is the foundation of a strong defense. A clean pass can set your team up for an effective attack.

Key Techniques

  • Platform: Create a flat surface by locking your elbows and joining your forearms.
  • Ready Stance: Knees bent, feet shoulder-width apart, and weight forward.
  • Angle Control: Tilt your platform to direct the ball toward the setter.

How to Receive Properly

  1. Anticipate: Watch the server’s body and toss to predict direction.
  2. Move Quickly: Adjust your position to get behind the ball.
  3. Angle & Pass: Use your platform to pass the ball high and toward the setter.

Tip:

Stay low and balanced. Don’t swing your arms—let the ball rebound off your platform with controlled direction.
