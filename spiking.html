Spiking the Ball

Spiking is the ultimate offensive weapon. It's all about timing, power, and placement.

Core Focus Areas

  • Approach: A fluid 3- or 4-step approach builds momentum.
  • Jump Timing: Time your jump based on the set’s height and speed.
  • Arm Swing: Pull back the hitting arm, then whip it forward with force.

How to Execute a Spike

  1. Get Ready: Start a few steps behind the attack line.
  2. Approach & Jump: Step-close-jump with explosive energy.
  3. Contact: Hit the ball at the peak of your jump with an open hand.
  4. Follow Through: Snap your wrist to drive the ball downward.

Tip:

Visualize your hit spot before you jump. Don’t just swing for power—aim with purpose!
